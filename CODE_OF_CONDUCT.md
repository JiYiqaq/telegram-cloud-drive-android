# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience or ability.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, assume good faith, respect privacy, accept technical correction, and focus criticism on code and ideas. Do not publish another person's private information or credentials.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, trolling, sustained disruption, impersonation, credential disclosure and other conduct inappropriate in a professional community are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct privately to the maintainer through the repository's configured private contact. Until a contact is configured, use GitHub's abuse-reporting mechanisms and do not expose sensitive personal information publicly. Maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions and participation that violate this policy.

This policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant 2.1 and adapted for this independently maintained project.
